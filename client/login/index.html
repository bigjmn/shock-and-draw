SHOCK & DRAW

Every round, each team has a designated drawer who tries to get the rest of the team to guess the team's given word.

Both teams play at the same time but players only see the canvas, word, and chat window for their own team.

When a word is guessed correctly, the team is given a point and assigned a new word. Each team tries to score as many points as possible before the round timer expires.

If a team guesses the word before the bonus timer expires, the opposing team is hit with an attack.

A drawer can also pass on a word, but doing so causes his own team to take an attack.

Each attack has a unique effect that hinders the attacked team in some way. The attack lasts until the attack timer expires (or until the end of the round.)

When all the rounds are complete, the team with the most points wins.

The total number of rounds is chosen before the start of the game.
